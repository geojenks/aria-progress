title: 3-D authoring widget now interactive on the page
---
The 3-D manifold widget is now hosted here as a self-contained static page, so a
reader can drive it directly — pick a gait preset, drag to orbit the sphere, and
watch the fingers make/break contact and drag the "juggling ball" round. It is the
same authoring front-end that streams to the MuJoCo bridge in the full tool; the
live physics link needs that local bridge, so on this page the "Connect" button is
inert by design (the preview itself runs entirely in the browser).
