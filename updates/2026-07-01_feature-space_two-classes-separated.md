The bending pneu-net family and the axially contracting/extending McKibben family
occupy distinct regions of the feature space, with the literature import and the rigid
Shadow-finger reference placed on the same axes. This is D3.1's central criterion —
at least two distinct actuator classes cleanly separated — met in simulation.
