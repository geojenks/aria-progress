The cross-actuator map is now an interactive page: switch either plotted axis, scrub
actuation level with a slider, click a datapoint to open its per-actuator "library
card", and multi-select actuators to compare side by side while the rest of the library
greys out. A front-end mock-up to be productionised; the data behind it is real
pipeline output.
