title: Library at 18 records — four simulated families plus five imports
---
The record library now holds 18 actuators through one shared schema: 13 simulated —
the pneu-net wall-thickness family (×4), the McKibben braid-angle family (×6), two
bellows (plain and slack-tendon variant), and a cable-driven finger — plus 5 imports:
three operating modes of a soft omnidirectional actuator digitised from the
literature, a rigid pneumatic cylinder, and a Shadow Hand finger as a rigid kinematic
reference. Every entry carries the same measured fields (mode spectrum, stroke,
blocked force, deformation composition), which is what lets simulation, literature
and rigid-reference devices sit on one set of axes in the atlas.
